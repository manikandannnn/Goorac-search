Differant between Browser and Search engine

A search engine is a special kind of website that helps users find web pages from other websites. ... Let's make it clear: A browser is a piece of software that retrieves and displays web pages; a search engine is a website that helps people find web pages from other websites
In searching mode you are looking for a specific result, while in browsing mode you are looking for general ideas or multiple answers. In searching mode you are looking to complete a task, while in browsing mode you may be looking for inspiration, entertainment, or just more information.
